Batman

Batman is a crime fighting vigilante in Gotham City. His true identity is billionare playboy Bruce Wayne who witnessed the murder of his parents as a child. He became Batman after swearing vengeance against criminals and to protect the weak. Several movies and series have been made depicting Batman such as The Dark Knight which is considered to be one of the greatest films of all time.
