Batman

Batman is a crime fighting vigilante in Gotham City. His true identity is billionare playboy Bruce Wayne who witnessed the murder of his parents as a child. He became Batman after swearing vengeance against criminals and to protect the innocent. Several movies and series have been made depicting Batman such as The Dark Knight which is considered to be one of the greatest films of all time.

  • One thing I admire about Batman is his method of defeating criminals. He has no superpowers and must rely on his strategy and intelligence to defeat them.
  • Another thing I admire is his purpose. He chose to protect the innocent and bring justice to those who have suffered a crime.
  • The last thing I admire is that anyone can be Batman. He is the most realistic superhero and anyone who chooses to do the right thing can be him.
